A basic "capture a flag" or "find a treasure box" model in which a "hidden" flag is placed on a m*n grid. The player has to capture the flag. When he/she does, a big reward is given; otherwise, a negative reward is imposed. One can play "manually". Or enable Q-learning - remember you may increase the simulation speed at the bottom. Say, x5 or x10 or x100 or x1000. Q-learning with stop at episode 2000. You may reduce to x1 after a few hundreds of episode as you may see the agent will play decently after some learning.
